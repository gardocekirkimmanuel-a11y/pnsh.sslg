About PNHS SSLG

The Puti-an National High School Supreme Secondary Learner Government is the official student leadership body dedicated to representing the voice, welfare, and aspirations of every learner. Through leadership, collaboration, and service, the PNHS SSLG aims to create meaningful initiatives that empower students and strengthen the school community.

Project PPP

Proteksyon, Pag-unlad, at Pagpapahalaga

Project PPP is the flagship initiative of the PNHS SSLG focused on learner welfare, student development, appreciation, and community-building through impactful and student-centered programs.

Proteksyon

Ensuring learner safety, welfare, and student support.

Pag-unlad

Promoting leadership, growth, and holistic development.

Pagpapahalaga

Strengthening unity, respect, and appreciation among learners.
